I am an Electronics and Signal Processing Engineer based in Rostock, Germany,
currently working as a Research Assistant in the Power Electronics group at the
University of Rostock. I hold an MSc in Information and Communication Technology
from FAU Erlangen-Nuremberg, with specialisation in digital and statistical signal
processing, machine learning in signal processing, and pattern recognition.

My research and industry experience spans data-driven analysis, Python and MATLAB
algorithm development, PCB design, and hardware characterisation — gained at
Infineon Technologies and Fraunhofer IISB. I have a peer-reviewed publication
at PCIM Europe 2025 and am actively seeking PhD and research positions in signal
processing, machine learning, and audio/biomedical applications.
